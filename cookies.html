This Cookie Policy explains how MatchupCoach.gg uses cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage) when you use our website. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. Similar technologies such as local storage store small amounts of data in the same way. Together we call these "cookies." They let a website remember your actions and preferences over time.

3. Your Consent and Choices

Where required by law, we ask for your consent before placing non-essential (performance/analytics and marketing) cookies, and you may withdraw consent at any time. Strictly necessary cookies are always active because the Service depends on them.

4. Managing Cookies

  • Browser controls. Most browsers let you view, block, or delete cookies in their settings. Search your browser's help for "cookies" or "site data." Blocking strictly necessary cookies may break login and other features.
  • Local storage. You can clear local storage via your browser's "clear site data" or developer tools.
  • Do Not Track. Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal. There is no industry standard for responding to it; we honor cookie choices as described above.
