Privacy Policy

MatchupCoach.gg · Last updated June 2026

MatchupCoach.gg ("MatchupCoach", "we") is a League of Legends matchup-coaching website. This policy explains what we collect and why. We keep it minimal on purpose.

What we collect

  • Account info. If you create an account with a username and password, we store your username and a securely hashed version of your password (we never store the password itself).
  • Sign in with Google. If you choose "Sign in with Google", Google sends us your basic profile (your name and email address) solely to create and identify your account. We do not request access to your Gmail, Drive, contacts, or any other Google data, and we cannot act on your Google account.
  • Plan status. Which plan (if any) is attached to your account.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not post anything to your Google account or read your email.
  • We do not show third-party advertising trackers.

How we use it

Only to log you in, keep you logged in across visits, and remember your plan. That's it.

Data storage & security

Account data is stored on our server. Passwords are hashed with PBKDF2. Sign-in sessions use random tokens that expire after 30 days.

Deleting your data

Email us (below) to delete your account and all associated data. We'll remove it promptly.

Children

MatchupCoach is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above.
